A linear search is the simplest searching method used to find a specific item in a list. It checks each element one by one from the beginning until it finds the target—or reaches the end.

🔍 Example

List: [4, 7, 9, 2, 5]
Target: 2

Steps:

♦️Check 4 → not 2
♦️Check 7 → not 2
♦️Check 9 → not 2
♦️Check 2 → ✔ found!
